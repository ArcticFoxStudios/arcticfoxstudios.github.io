What we offer

Tad Offers you the ability to get your CWP in a fun hands on enviorment for beginners as well as the more advanced shooters where you can handle many pistols and revolvers to feel which one will be right for you. Tad provides the guns and the ammo for the class or you can bring your own gun and use that. After completion of the class you'll be ready to send away for you CWP.

With this class you will learn the following:

  • Gun saftey rules
  • The important parts of a pistol and revolver
  • Ammunition and how it works
  • Safe gun handeling and operation
  • Proper gun cleaning
  • Proper gun and ammunition storage
  • Proper sight allignment
  • Proper loading and unloading of pistols and revolvers
  • South Carolina gun laws and where you can legally carry
  • How to properly draw from concealment
  • Proper carry and storage of your firearm in your vehicle
  • What you should teach your children about gun saftey

*Classes are roughly 8 hours long beginning at 8am and ending at 5pm.
